About RoCham Malaysia

Established to serve as a vital bridge between the Romanian and Malaysian business communities, RoCham Malaysia provides a robust platform for networking, collaboration, and mutual growth. We are committed to facilitating market entry, promoting investment opportunities, and advocating for the interests of our members in both countries.

Our mission encompasses:

  • Promoting and expanding bilateral trade and investment.
  • Providing comprehensive market insights and business intelligence.
  • Organizing impactful networking events and B2B matchmaking sessions.
  • Advocating for a favorable and transparent business environment.
  • Fostering cultural understanding and exchange initiatives.

Our Services

Investment Facilitation

Supporting businesses with guidance and connections for investment opportunities in Romania and Malaysia.

Business Matchmaking

Connecting companies with reliable partners, distributors, and clients for cross-border collaborations.

Advocacy & Liaison

Representing members' interests and liaising with government bodies and institutions.

Events & Networking

Hosting seminars, workshops, and exclusive networking events to foster community and knowledge sharing.

Consultancy & Advice

Offering expert advice on legal, administrative, and operational aspects of doing business internationally.
