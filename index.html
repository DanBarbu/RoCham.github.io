About RoCham Malaysia

Established to serve as a vital bridge between the Romanian and Malaysian business communities, RoCham Malaysia provides a robust platform for networking, collaboration, and mutual growth. We are committed to facilitating market entry, promoting investment opportunities, and advocating for the interests of our members in both countries.

Our mission encompasses:

  • Promoting bilateral trade and investment.
  • Providing market insights and business intelligence.
  • Organizing networking events and B2B matchmaking.
  • Advocating for a favorable business environment.
  • Fostering cultural understanding and exchange.

Our Services

Market Entry Support

Guidance and resources for businesses looking to enter or expand in Romanian or Malaysian markets.

B2B Matchmaking

Connecting businesses with potential partners, suppliers, and clients in both countries.

Advocacy & Representation

Representing members' interests to government bodies and promoting a conducive business climate.

Events & Networking

Regular events, seminars, and workshops to foster connections and share insights.

Business Intelligence

Providing up-to-date information on market trends, regulations, and economic developments.

Certification & Endorsement

Supporting businesses with official documentation and endorsement for cross-border activities.
